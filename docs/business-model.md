**RentPath Business Model & Pricing Strategy**

---

## 1. Monetization Philosophy

RentPath will generate revenue by offering premium services to property managers while maintaining free access for renters.
This aligns with market expectations and encourages platform adoption from both sides.

---

## 2. Core Principles

- **Free for renters**: To maintain trust and reduce friction
- **Value-based tiers for property managers**: Monetize via time savings, trust-building features, and professional tools
- **Scalable pricing**: Plans designed for individuals, teams, and enterprises
- **No feature walls on trust**: Verification and applications remain usable at all tiers

---

## 3. Pricing Plans

| Plan           | Price/mo | Target Users                             | Key Features                                                             |
| -------------- | -------- | ---------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Starter**    | €0       | Private landlords testing the platform   | 1-3 listings, basic invites, manual verification                         |
| **Pro**        | €29      | Small agencies or professional landlords | Unlimited listings, branded invites, priority verification, export tools |
| **Team**       | €79      | Agencies with multiple managers          | Multi-manager support, role permissions, analytics, application CRM      |
| **Enterprise** | Custom   | Enterprise agencies, white-label clients | SLA, API access, custom features, onboarding support                     |

---

## 4. Faster Verification as a Premium Feature

“Faster verification” is included in Pro+ plans and is defined as:

- **Priority queue** for document review
- **Automated pre-screening** to flag errors before human review
- **Time block prioritization**: faster turnaround for paid accounts
- **Immediate rejection feedback**: reduce resubmission cycles

---

## 5. Transactional Revenue (Future Phase)

- **Rent collection & deposit handling**: Small transaction fees
- **Third-party services**: Insurance, identity checks, background screening via partnerships
- **E-signature & lease management**: Per-use fees or add-ons

---

## 6. Enterprise and Custom Integrations

For larger clients:

- Custom onboarding and account support
- API for syncing listings, teams, and data
- Dedicated domain or white-label version
- Optional compliance/legal modules (SLA, GDPR+)

---

## 7. Free Plan Strategy

- Allows onboarding without friction
- Encourages growth via word-of-mouth and invitation links
- Acts as a trial funnel into paid plans by exposing limitations (e.g. listing cap, slower verification)

---

## 8. Long-Term Model Expansion

- In-app upsells (priority boosts, extra users)
- Paid document vault for renters (reusable verification kits)
- Licensing RentPath tech as a SaaS platform for agencies outside target markets
