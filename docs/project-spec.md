# **RentPath Project Specification**

---

## 1. Vision

RentPath is a digital platform designed to streamline the residential rental process in Europe. It connects property managers (private landlords or agencies) with prospective renters, simplifying communication, document exchange, and the rental application journey. The platform emphasizes trust and transparency by enabling identity and income verification for both parties.

---

## 2. Target Audience

### Property Managers

- Private individuals who own rental property
- Real estate agents or agencies managing listings on behalf of owners
- Teams that manage properties collaboratively

### Renters

- Young professionals, students, expats
- Individuals searching for verified rental listings
- Tenants needing a structured way to apply for properties and submit necessary documentation

---

## 3. Problem Statement

Renting a home in Europe can be fragmented, opaque, and time-consuming.
Property managers struggle with unqualified applicants, and renters face inconsistent expectations, paperwork, and communication.
There is a need for a unified, transparent, and efficient system.

---

## 4. Value Proposition

RentPath offers:

- A secure, invite-driven application process
- Identity and income verification tools to build trust
- A clean experience tailored to the European rental ecosystem

---

## 5. Key Features

- **Dual Portals**: Separate onboarding and experience for property managers and renters
- **Verified Profiles**: Document-based identity and income verification for both parties
- **Invite-Based Applications**: Renters can apply using shareable or direct invitation links
- **Multi-Manager Support**: Multiple managers can collaborate on property listings
- **Application Tracking**: Renters track status; managers manage submissions

---

## 6. Geographic Focus

Initial rollout will target Luxembourg, where strict documentation and verification are common rental expectations.
This allows RentPath to offer real, localized value right from launch.
In a second phase we will target neighbouring countries: France, Geramny, Belgium, Netherlands, ...
Final phase would be european market as a whole.

---

## 7. Business Model (Initial Concept)

- Freemium model with core features free
- Optional paid plans for property managers (e.g. more listings, advanced screening tools, branding, ...)
- Future integration of subscription tier and commission based services such as rent collection, report generation, insurance deals, ...

---

## 8. Phased Approach

In the first phase, RentPath will focus on solving the most critical pain points: the verification and rental application process.
This includes making it easy and secure for renters to submit documentation and for property managers to evaluate and verify applicants.
In later phases, RentPath aims to become the platform that digitizes the **entire rental lifecycle** — from listing and application to lease signing, rent payment, maintenance tracking, and move-out.

## 9. Long-Term Vision

- Become the go-to trusted platform for renting across Europe
- Automate most of the rental workflow (documents, screening, lease signing)
- Partner with financial, insurance, and relocation services
- Expand into commercial and short-term rentals
